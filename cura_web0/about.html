About Cura

Our mission is simple: to emotionally support people living with chronic illness by giving them a gentle, private, and accessible companion for daily life.

Mission

Cura was designed with the understanding that chronic illness affects not just the body, but the mind. We aim to provide a calm digital space for reflection, coping strategies, and reminders that help make care feel more manageable.

Values

  • Compassion-first design
  • Privacy and local-first storage
  • Accessibility for diverse users
  • Small, meaningful interactions
